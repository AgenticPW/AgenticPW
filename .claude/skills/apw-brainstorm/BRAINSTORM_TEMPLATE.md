# Brainstorm Template

Copy this structure into your work item's **`brainstorm.md`** artifact — see `apw-work-with-work-item-artifacts` for where work-item artifacts live. Capture the
*whole* idea log, not just the winner — the discarded options are the value a
future reader would otherwise have to regenerate.

```markdown
# Brainstorm: <challenge>

- **Date:** <YYYY-MM-DD>
- **Status:** diverging | converging | complete
- **Slug:** <task-short-slug>

## Challenge

The goal, in one sentence.

### Constraints
- **Hard:** ... (genuinely fixed)
- **Assumed:** ... (taken as fixed, but worth challenging)

### Success criteria
What a winning idea must do — the yardstick convergence measures against.

### Re-framings
The same problem stated 2–3 different ways, to break the anchor:
- Broadened: ...
- Narrowed: ...
- Inverted: ...

## Idea log

Every idea generated, with the lens that produced it. Judgment deferred —
"bad" ideas stay in the log.

| # | Idea | Lens | Note |
|---|------|------|------|
| 1 | ... | first-principles | ... |
| 2 | ... | inversion | ... |
| 3 | ... | cross-domain analogy | ... |

> Lenses: first-principles, cross-domain analogy, inversion, constraint
> removal, constraint addition, persona shift, SCAMPER.

### Past the first cluster
Ideas that broke out of the safe default — the contrarian ones, or those that
challenge an **assumed** constraint:
- ...

## Clusters

The ideas grouped into themes, so the shape of the space is visible.

- **Theme A:** ideas #…
- **Theme B:** ideas #…

## Evaluation

The strongest candidates scored against the success criteria. Be honest about
the downsides.

| Idea | Pros | Cons | Meets criteria? |
|------|------|------|-----------------|
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

### Killed
Ideas that didn't survive, and why:
- ... — reason
- ... — reason

## Shortlist & recommendation

The ranked shortlist or single recommendation, with reasoning. For each, say
what it needs next: research (`apw-research`), a prototype, or implementation
(`apw-implement`).

## Open questions

- Assumptions that still need confirming.
- What couldn't be resolved during ideation.
```
